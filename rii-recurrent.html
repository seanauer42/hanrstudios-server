RII Recurrent

  1. FAR part 121 governs what
    Operating requirements: Domestic Flag and Supplemental Operators
  2. You have been issued a workcard containing several steps as follows...
    The AMT signs for the "M" blocks, a qualified member of Aircraft Quality Control Department signs the "I", and the RII signs of the "RII" block
  3. When will a maintenance action be classified as a Required Inspection Item(RII)?
    By maintnance before the work begins
  4. Per GMM 7-1-100, only QC management and QC Inspectors are authorized to designate any maintenance task as RII
    True
  5. FedEx Express Engineering Order Work Instruction Cards (E.O's) and Work Instruction Cards have skill levels and RII requirements identified
    True
  6. You are accomplishing a task which requires the replacement of a cargo door latching/locking...
    Yes, the installation, rigging, and test require RII buyback
  7. Who can perform the "EQI" inspection?
    ETOPS QC Inspector
  8. As an RII Inspector you may designate a maintenance task as required inspection item only with the approval of maintenance control
    False
  9. You are accomplishing a maintenance task of an RII identified system/componenet...
    No, because GMM 7-1-100 Table 1 states: Rig/Adjust/Test...
  10. All RII's must have 2 signatures, one from a properly certified mechanic who performed the work, and on authorized to buy-back the task
    True
  11. As an RII Inspector designee having authorization to perfom a buy-back on a 767 aircraft is also authorized to perform buy-backs on a 757 aircraft
    False
  12. How is the maintenance action denoted as an RII on the Aircraft Maintenance Log page
    Denoted by checking the "YES/NO" block marked RII for that discrepancy
  13. Who can perform the "EQM" inspection?
    ETOPS AMT
  14. Can an RII authorized AMT accomplish work within a FeEx Express Work Instruction Card...
    Yes, provided that the RII authorized AMT, accomplishing the buy-back, did not do any part of the work step/item being bought-back
  15. As an aircraft mechanic, you assume the position of an RII inspecotr designee...
    True
  16. When an RII authorized AMT is working a repair, via an Engineering Authorization(E.A.), FACTing the EAML...
    False, an E.A does not have the RII requirements pre-defined and must be determined before work begins...
  17. How often must FAR and RII recurrent training be completed in oreder the remain current?
    Annually
  18. All steps of an AMM task/sub-task that have a flashing red RII indicator require buyback.
    False. Only the items/actions identified within Table 1 of GMM 7-1-100 require RII buyback
  19. An engine change has been accomplished per a FedEx Express Work Instruction Card...
    NO, because a FedEx Express Work Instruction Card has the skill levels and RII requirements identified for individual steps/items
  20. Who can perform the "I" Inspection
    Aircraft Quality Control Inspector
