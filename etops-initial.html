  1. The aircraft just came out of a "B-Check". Which statement is true regarding the Pre-Departure Service Check
    A PDSC is required prior to the ETOPS flight because th ePDSC is not precluded by any other check
  2. What is the preferred method used to service engine oils
    One EQ AMT services the left engine, and another EQ AMT services the right engine
  3. While not all 767s are operated as ETOPS, all 767s are maintained to support ETOPS. There is a process, controlled by MOC and Engineering that will allow for any FedEx 767 to become one of the ETOPS Operations fleet
    True
  4. With regard to ETOPS, primary oversight responsibility is a function of
    AMT
  5. Where can you go to find answers to your ETOPS questions
    All of these
  6. Engine oils may be serviced upon arrival, even thought the PDSC may not be accomplished until a later time
    True
  7. The ETOPS portion of the GMM defines how FedEx will conduct its maintenance to support ETOPS. All FedEx personnel involved in, or with the ETOPS program will find this information in GMM chapter
    15
  8. A PDSC was accomplished at 0200z on an aircraft originally scheduled to depart at 0400z...
    • Yes, the PDSC will need to accomplished per GMM 15-6-000
    • Yes, the PDSC will need to accomplished per GMM 15-1-000
  9. Engine oil
    Must be serviced to "FULL" at the engine oil tank, prior to ETOPS operation
  10. Which document is used to ensure that only ETOPS parts are install on ETOPS airplanes
    IPC
  11. The computer system is down. How can you you calculate oil consumption
    The PDSC has instructions for manually calculating oil consumption
  12. A Pre-Departure Service Check
    The final sign off, signifying the PDSC is accomplished, MUST be by a FedEx Express ETOPS qualified AMT
  13. You have been tasked with replacement of the left and right engine fuel filters, due to a significant tank entry

    Yes, annually

    True, but only with MOC approval, and the aircraft must not fly ETOPS
  17. On FedEx forms ETOPS tasks will be identified by which of the following?
    By the letters "EQ"

    Repair or defer the APU. Once repairs havae been accomplished, a verification flight must be performed per GMM 15-11-015
