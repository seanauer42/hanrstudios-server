Environmental Quiz Above ground storage tanks

  1. After the AST is installed, who is responsible for operation, compliance inspections, and record keeping
    location management
  2. When correspondence is received from a regulatory agency pertaining to registration or payment of fees for ASTs, it should be forwarded to Finance for processing
    False
  3. As per FedEx Express policy, ASTs are STATIONARY and have a capacity equal to or greater than 55 gallons
    True
  4. As per FedEx Express policy, containers are PORTABLE OR MOVEABLE and have a capacity equal to or greater than 55 gallons
    True
  5. ASTs and specified containers must be monitored and inspected for
    Leaks, spills, and overall integrity
  6. At SPCC locations, AST inspection requirements are contained in the SPCC plan
    True
  7. At SPCC locations, where should the AST Inspection Logs be maintained?
    in the SPCC plan
  8. At Non-SPCC locations, ASTs and every container with a capacity LARGER than 55 gallons containing fuel, must be inspected at least monthly using the required EMD form
    True
  9. The monthly AST Inspection forms for non-SPCC locations can be found on
    EMD website
  10. At non-SPCC locations, where should the AST Inspection Logs be maintainted
    in the central Location Environmental File
  11. Match the vessels with the appropriate monitoring and inspection requirement
    • Every container [3]
    • Every AST [1]
    • ASTs and containers contained petroleum [2]
  12. ASTs must have some form of containment
    True
  13. Glycol, rainfall and snowmelt found in secondary containment around an AST can be released, because it is not harmful to the environment
    False
  14. Vessels deployed TEMPORARILY to store during emergency events
    A&B above
  15. One of the most common reasons that companies receive a fine or penalty related to ASTs is
    lack of adequate documentation verifying compliance with inspection requirements
  16. In the event that an AST needs to be closed or removed from service, who would you contact for assistance
    EMD
