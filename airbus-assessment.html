  1. After moving the #1 NAV Selector to the NAV position and noticing the BATT OPER light is illuminated from 3 to 10 seconds, what is the next step?
    Verify the ALIGN MODE legend illuminates while the BATT OPER legend extinguishes.
  2. After moving the Display Selector to the HDG/STS position, what is the next step?
    Move the #1 MSU knob to the NAV position
  3. What Happens when you move the door switch in the down position when the door is open (70 degree position) and the hook is locked in place?
    NOT The door closes
  4. After applying external power and pressing in the 3 battery P/BSW to place the batteries in the auto position, what is the next step to align the IRU's?
    Move the System Display rotary knob to system #1
  5. After moving the System Display rotary knob to system #1 position, what is the next step?
    Move the Display Selector to the HDG/STS position
  6. After pressing the blue PTU switch, what is the next step?
    Wait for the blue AND green hydraulic system pressures to stabilize
  7. Which Knob, Lever, or device is used to direct fluid into the Yellow Reservoir during hydraulic replenishment
    Label A
  8. How do you reduce the yellow hydraulic system quantity and raise the accumulator pressure on the triple indicator at the same time
    Press and hold the parking brake accumulator pressure switch
  9. What is the process of turning off external power?
    Turn off all three battery P/BSW, then release out the EXT PWR P/BSW.
  10. After pressing the yellow PTU switch, what is the next step?
    Wait for the three (2) hydraulic system pressures to stabilize.
  11. According to the FedEx JRH, the first APU start of the day requires that the AMT perform an Auto Extinguishing test of the Fire Detection System before starting the APU.
    NOT Label C
  12. What test is initiated when pressing the 3WZ P/BSW next to the Captain's Main Altimeter?
    Level 1 Self-Test of the EGPWS
  13. Viewing the 438VU VENT Panel, which statement is correct?
    Air is flowing overboard, therefore External Power was properly applied to the aircraft and Avionics Cooling is proper
  14. Using the illustration provided of the Brake Yellow Pressure Triple Indicator (71GG), will the FWD, AFT and/or MCD door open when the operation lever is moved to OPEN?
    NOT No. The Triple Indicator has nothing to do with door operation. Just follow normal door operating procedures.
  15. After moving the Locking Lever "handle" to the down position what should you do?
    Make sure the vent door is open
  16. After pressing the P/BSW on the ECAM Control Panel, the HYD page is displayed on the R ECAM DU. What is the next maintenance step to follow in order to apply hydraulic power?
    Obtain clearance from ground and turn on the beacon.
  17. During an automatic Annunciator Light Test, a faulty annunciator light (failed lamp) was detected and the test ceased. What knob, P/BSW, or lever does the AMT activate to continue the test?
    Label D
  18. When performing the trouble shooting the FADEC system on number one engine, the AMT views the following on the L ECAM DU. What does the FADEC test show the AMT?
    The AMT may not dispatch the aircraft because the #1 engine is in a "non-dispatchable" state. The AMT must troubleshoot per the TSM.
  19. After pressing the green electric hydraulic pump switch and waiting for the LO PR legend to extinguish, what is the next step?
    Wait for the green hydraulic system pressure to reach 3000 psi +/- 150 before proceeding to the next step.
  20. After external power is turned on and Gen 1 & Gen 2 amber fault legends are illuminated, make sure there are no other white lights illuminated other than the battery switches. What is the next step?
    Make sure equipment cooling vent panel shows overboard venting.
