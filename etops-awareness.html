ETOPS Awareness

  1. What is ETOPS acronym for
    Extended Operations
  2. What MEL is used to reestrict ETOPS status due to ETOPS Significant System Maintenance perfomed by non-ETOPS qualified personnel
    MEL 20-05
  3. ETOPS Qualification is required to simply service the APU oil on ETOPS 777
    True
  4. ETOPS Qualification is required to accomplish a maintenance task idntified with the letters (EQ) AMT
    True
  5. Where in the GMM can you determine if ETOPS...
    GMM 15-3-0
  6. Where in the GMM can you determine which FedEx A/C combinations are authorized for ETOPS
    GMM 15-2-0
  7. How are FedEx ETOPS aircraft identified from the ramp focal view
    The word ETOPS in black letters on the Nose Gear door
  8. After completion of this course, you will be ETOPS qualified
    False
  9. For FedEx twin engine A/C...
    60
  10. GMM chapter ___ contains the ETOPS Maintenance program
    15
