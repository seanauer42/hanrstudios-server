767 Recurrent

  1. During which test do both the control wheels slowly turn to the right and then to the left?
    Aileron Servo Test
  2. During the Flight Director(FD) switch tests, you need to verify whether the pitch bars are UP in the Captain's...
    Yes
  3. During testing, once the autopilot disengages and the alarm sounds, what button should you press to silence the alarm?
    Either of the two A/P Disconnect buttons.
  4. To run the AUTOLAND test, you should ________
    Attach an MCDP remote control unit to the maintenance port
  5. According to the Flight Displays shown, are the IRUs aligned?
    Yes
  6. Where are the A/P Engage Switches located on the Flight Deck shown?
    B
  7. Where is the Captain's Flight Director(FD) Source Select Switch located on the forward panel shown?
    A
  8. Once the MCDP is powered ON and switched to display Ground Tests, what switch is used to scroll up or down through the available ground tests?
    D
  9. Where is the Autoland Status Annunciator (ASA) located on the forward panel shown?
    B
  10. When running the Bus Isolation Test, the EICAS Display becomes inoperative and once the display comes back ON...
    Press the CONF/MCDP pushbutton
  11. Once you are finished with the Autoland test, simply unplugging the MCDP Remote Control Unit, switches the MCDP to OFF
    False
  12. Which of the following is NOT an additional required LLM system required for conducting LLM operations on a B767 aircraft?
    Fuel Quantity Systems
  13. Where is the Autothrottle (A/T) Switch located on the portion of the forward panel shown?
    B
  14. After performing maintenance on an LLM autoland component, which of the following ground test is required to certify the aircraft as LLM capable?
    30 CURRENT FAULT REPORT
